SLAY Festival

Welcome to your SLAY!

SLAY Festival is a learning, networking, and super fun experience for the young
African woman.Since 2017, we have successfully curated a celebration of innovation,
culture and community in the professional, business, and general lifestyle spaces for
young African women. Join us at the next SLAY Festival and learn from incredible
speakers, attend transformational masterclasses, shop from the best young
entrepreneurs, build your Pan-African network, listen to the livest music and hang out
with your favorite brands.
