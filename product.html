Pass Me The Mic Course with Darling

About The Course

“Pass Me the Mic” is a free, 30-minute, online course created to help
young African women learn communication skills to advance their
careers. This course is a fast, free, self-guided course designed to help
build your communication skills for work.

Identify and Explore Proven Strategies

With the Pass Me The Mic course, you will learn:

  • How to craft your professional brand
  • How to communicate your worth assertively, pitching your values,
    especially during job interviews and also while negotiating your
    salary
  • Smart negotiating strategies to get the best job offer!
