  • Trainings to become a better writer and editor

Become an Occasional Contributor

  • Got good writing skills?
  • Got an interesting article you’d like to publish?
  • Want to contribute every now and then without the commitment?

Become an SLA Contributing Writer

3-months commitment

  • Expected to write at least 2 assigned articles a month
  • All articles must fit with theme of the editorial calendar
  • Must write articles in the following categories – Business & Finance, Innovation & Startups, Lifestyle & Culture, Work & Productivity
  • Cannot pitch articles

6-months commitment

  • Priority publishing
  • Expected to write at least 2 articles a month
  • Can pitch articles that fit with the theme of the editorial calendar
  • First consideration for SLA community opportunities

1-year commitment

  • Priority publishing
  • Expected to write at least 1 articles a month
  • Can pitch special and editorial-themes articles
  • First consideration and access to exclusive SLA community opportunities
  • Periodic writers’ training opportunities
