Some of Our Core Values

#Excellence

We believe in consistently bringing the highest
standards to our work and consistently
pushing ourselves to do better and be better.
We believe that every job is a self-portrait of
the person who did it. Therefore we
autograph our work with excellence.

#WeStayLearning

We are committed to continual growth and
#WeStayLearning. We know that growth is
hard but we’re still committed to leaning into
it.

#Level2Thinking

We are thoughtful, reflective and think deeply
about how we work. We do that second or
even third level of thinking to fully understand
the problem and come up with an excellent
solution.

#Resilience

We understand that trying to take over the
world is hard work and we’re willing to get it
done. We recognise that we’ll have bad days
at work where we’ll be pushed to our limit.
But guess what? When we get knocked down,
we take a deep breath, eat some ice cream
and just get back to business.

#Commercially-minded

We understand that though we have a social
mission, we are a business and we need to
keep the lights on. We are always careful to
understand the financial implications of our
actions and initiatives.
